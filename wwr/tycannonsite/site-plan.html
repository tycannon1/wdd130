Content

Home page

Why will people come to my site? People will come to my website to feel inspired and motivated to take part in learning about travel and destinations I have been to and find out the ways I go about traveling. In the past I have shared content of unique places and experiences to where I have traveled. People have always reached out through messages and emails to find out information on these places, with a website in place I can share that information in one spot for everyone to see! Visitors for my site will be aided by social media growth, When viewing my social media they can be directed to my website to find out more information. The appeal from my photographs and visual media will create an aesthetic on the website that will hopefully be enticing and pleasing to the eye, that will also help users gravitate towards my site.
