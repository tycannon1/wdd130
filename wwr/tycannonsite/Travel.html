GARTHS BOULDER GARDENS.

Here is a hidden oasis in Palm Desert, Located in southern california Garths BOULDER Gardens is a camping/airbnb sanctuary built in the boulders of the desert. There are lots of outdoor furniture and outdoor pools built into the bolders.

MONORE HOTSPRINGS.

Monroe hotsprings consist of bath tubs build into rocks that are connected to hotsprings. I came to these hotsprings after a marathon in Saint George and it was the greatest!! Very cool and unique.
